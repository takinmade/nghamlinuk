About Us

Company Profile

NG Hamlin UK was birthed in 2021 as part of our vision to expand internationally. Our parent company in Nigeria has a long and storied history in consulting and have been pioneers in the industry for many years and are known for taking innovative steps. We took a leap to explore business opportunities in the United Kingdom slowly introducing ourselves and seeking business solutions for small to medium companies (SME’s). Today we are slowly navigating and introducing our robust Business solutions Consultancy services such as Data Management, Document Management, Project Management, IT Trainings, Cloud Services and Web Designs.

We are very proud of our achievements in a short period of time. Information Technology completely changes the way we analyse and present data. We have embraced new technologies and have ensured that our clients receive cutting edge analytics. As we go on towards the future, we intend to exploit the full potential of new technologies to power our services.

Our Mission: To approach every client’s business as if it were our own. We believe a consulting firm should be more than an advisor. We put ourselves in our clients’ shoes, align our incentives with their objectives, and collaborate to unlock the full potential of their business. This builds deep and enjoyable relationships.

Company Values: Safety, Innovation, Trust (SIT)
